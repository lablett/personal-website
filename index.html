About Me

I currently work as a data scientist, focusing on the development of statistical and machine learning models to extract value from geological and operational data in the mining and metals industry.

Also trained in geology and geophysics, I previously worked as a Mine Geologist during the start-up phase of First Quantum Minerals’ Sentinel Cu (Ni, Co) mine in NW Zambia. Whilst there, I was exposed to a range of cutting-edge technologies, including the use of AI for extracting hidden patterns in geological data. Inspired by this experience, I went on to study for an MSc in Spatio-Temporal Analytics and Big Data Mining at University College London, which included the study of both data science and software development.

Software development is something I very much enjoy, and complete programming courses in my spare time. This website was created by me after taking a course in fullstack web development.
