About Me

I am a data scientist and geologist with and interest in software development, and an MSc in Spatio-Temporal Analytics and Big Data Mining from University College London. I am currently focusing on the development of statistical and machine learning models to extract value from geological and operational data in the mining and metals industry.

As a quick-learner with a passion for programming, I enjoy learning new languages, algorithms, and technologies to best serve the business use case and communicate results to clients. This website was created using skills learnt from Andrei Neagoie's course The Complete Web Developer in 2019: Zero to Mastery, which I am in the process of completing.

Outside of programming, I enjoy reading science fiction, playing logic and 3D puzzle games, hosting dinner parties, and exploring the countryside.
